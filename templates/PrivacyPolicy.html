Privacy Policy

At Online Wine World, we are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you visit or make a purchase from our store.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Shipping and billing addresses
  • Payment information (handled securely through third-party providers)
  • Purchase history
  • Website usage data (e.g., pages visited, time spent)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • To process and fulfill your orders
  • To provide customer support
  • To improve our website and services
  • For marketing purposes: We may use your name and email address to send you promotional offers, newsletters, and product recommendations based on your preferences and purchase history.
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. Marketing and Communication

By providing your personal data, you agree that we may use it to send you marketing messages by email or SMS. You may opt out at any time by clicking the "unsubscribe" link in our emails or contacting us directly.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We may share your information only with:

  • Trusted third-party service providers (e.g., payment processors, shipping companies)
  • Authorities when legally required

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.
